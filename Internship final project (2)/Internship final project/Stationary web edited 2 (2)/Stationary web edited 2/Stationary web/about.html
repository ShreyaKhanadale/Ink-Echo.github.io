Overview of Ink & Echo

Ink & Echo was founded with a vision to revolutionize the stationery industry by providing innovative products and exceptional customer service. Our journey began with a small team of passionate individuals dedicated to delivering quality stationery solutions to our community.
Ink & Echo is proudly owned by Shreya Khanadale, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for creativity and innovation. With Shreya Khanadale at the helm, Ink & Echo has grown into a trusted name in the stationery industry, known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
There are several ways to reach out to Ink & Echo. You can visit any of our four branches in Dharwad, contact us via phone or email, or connect with us on social media. Our dedicated team is always ready to assist you with your stationery needs.
To avail offers at Ink & Echo, simply visit any of our branches and inquire about our current promotions and discounts. Our friendly staff will be happy to assist you in finding the best deals on stationery products.
Downloading the Ink & Echo app is simple! Visit the App Store or Google Play Store, search for "Ink & Echo", and download the app to your smartphone or tablet. With our app, you can easily browse our catalog, place orders, and stay updated on the latest promotions and offers.
